The Best Movies According to Edmund

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope

From go to whoa this movie kept me enthralled. A wonderful story that captures your imagination.

Top Gun: Maverick

The first Top Gun was good, this one is even better. Was never a big fan of Tom Cruise but this one changed my mind.

The Great Escape

One of the all time classics. Steve McQueen is a legend and proves his star quality in this film. Based on true events in WWII, I recommend this to everyone.
